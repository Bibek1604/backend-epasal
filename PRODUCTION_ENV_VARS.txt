## STEPS TO DEPLOY:

1. Go to: https://render.com/dashboard
2. Click: "New +" → "Web Service"
3. Connect: Your GitHub repository (Bibek1604/backend-epasal)
4. Render will auto-detect render.yaml
5. In Settings → Environment, add ALL variables above
6. Replace:
   - MONGODB_URI_PROD with your MongoDB Atlas production URI
   - CORS_ORIGIN with your frontend domain
   - API_BASE with your Render service URL
7. Click "Create Web Service"
8. Wait for deploy (2-5 minutes)
